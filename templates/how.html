How to use the app

Getting started

Welcome to the app! Here's a quick guide on how to use it:

  1. Upload your photo by clicking on the "Choose file" button.
  2. Select the operation you want to perform by clicking on the corresponding button (e.g. "Convert to grayscale").
  3. Wait for the operation to finish. This may take a few seconds depending on the size of your photo.
  4. Download your processed photo by clicking on the "Download" button.

Supported operations

The app currently supports the following operations:

  • Convert to grayscale: This operation converts your photo to black and white.
  • Detect faces: This operation detects the faces in your photo and draws a rectangle around each face.
  • Convert to PNG/JPEG/WEBP: This operation converts your photo to the selected file format.
